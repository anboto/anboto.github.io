OfficeAutomation

Includes classes to handle office programs like spreadsheets and text editors:

OfficeDoc

OfficeSheet

 

Supported office suites are Open/LibreOffice and Microsoft Office.

 

OfficeAutomation is implemented only in U++ Windows version.

 

Some Microsoft Excel functions have been kindly provided by Ratah.
